tl;dr

Locations contain Cells which contain Rooms.

Rooms contain Characters and Furniture.

Characters and Furniture can contain Items.

Characters can wear Clothing, which are extended Items.

Rooms, Characters, Items(, Clothing), and Furniture are all Entities.

An entity has an ID, Name, Description, and Owner.

Yes, a Character can be owned by another Character.

//[js/data/Locations.js]
